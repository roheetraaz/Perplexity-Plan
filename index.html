Learning Techniques Guide

Active Recall

Very High Effectiveness Daily, 15-20 minutes

Testing yourself without looking at answers first

Implementation:

  • Close your notes and write down what you remember
  • Use flashcards for key concepts
  • Practice explaining without referring to materials

Spaced Repetition

Very High Effectiveness Daily review, weekly comprehensive

Reviewing material at increasing intervals

Implementation:

  • Review new material after 1 day, then 3 days, then 1 week
  • Use apps like Anki for systematic scheduling
  • Focus on concepts that are harder to remember

Feynman Technique

High Effectiveness Weekly for major concepts

Explaining concepts in simple terms

Implementation:

  • Choose a concept and explain it in simple terms
  • Identify gaps in your understanding
  • Go back to source material to fill gaps
  • Simplify language and use analogies

Pomodoro Technique

High Effectiveness Throughout daily study sessions

25-minute focused work sessions with breaks

Implementation:

  • Set a timer for 25 minutes of focused work
  • Take a 5-minute break after each session
  • Take a longer 15-30 minute break after 4 sessions
  • Track completed pomodoros
